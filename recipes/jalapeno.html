Jalapeno

Description

A very hot green chilli pepper, used especially in Mexican-style cooking.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium jalapeno peppers, halved and seeded
  • 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ounce shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste
  • 3 slices salami
  • 6 slices bacon, cut in half lengthwise

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (199 degrees C) according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Fill each jalapeno half with some cream cheese and Cheddar cheese. Place 1/2 a slice of salami on top of each pepper, wrap with a piece of bacon, and secure with a toothpick, if necessary.
  3. Layer jalapeno poppers inside of the preheated air fryer and cook until bacon is browned and cheese is melted, 10 to 12 minutes.
